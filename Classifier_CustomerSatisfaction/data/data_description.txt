DESCTRIPTION OF SOME FIELDS
USER_ID - The userid of customer
IS_SATISFIED - This is the field the we like to understand (0-not satisfied 1-satisfied)
AGE- age of the customer
ZODIAC_SIGN- zodiac sign of the customer
OWN_HOUSE - Does the customer rents or owns a house (O - own, R -rent, NA - no information)
TOT_REG_PHONE - Total phone numbers registered
PAYMENT_TYPE- Payment frequency of the customer
LOAN_WAITING - is the loan pending
LOAN_REJECT - has the customer withdrawn the loan application 
IOS_USER- Has the user used an iphone
ADRIOD_USER - Has the user used a android based phone
IS_REFERRED - Has the user referred
CREDIT_SCORE - Customer's credit score
